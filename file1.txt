Actors Rashmika Mandanna and Vijay Deverakonda shared the first official
pictures from their wedding. The two got married in dual traditional ceremonies 
on Thursday in Udaipur.
The couple looked regal in their traditional outfits for the two ceremonies.
While the bride chose to wear an embellished traditional saree as per the 
Kodava customs, the groom looked dapper in his traditional Mundu.
